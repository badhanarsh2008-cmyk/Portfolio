FolioNet

I realized that a lot of developers build amazing things, but most of those projects never reach real users. Not because the product is bad — people simply don’t know they exist. I’ve faced this myself while building apps and experimenting with different ideas. That’s why I’m building FolioNet — a platform where developers can showcase the tools, apps, and services they create, and let other people actually discover and use them. The goal is to help builders get visibility, connect with users, and turn side projects into something real instead of letting them stay hidden inside GitHub folders or local servers.
